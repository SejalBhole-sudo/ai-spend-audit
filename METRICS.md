# Metrics

## North Star Metric

### Monthly Qualified Audits

A “qualified audit” means:
- The user completes the audit
- Has meaningful AI spend
- Shows potential savings opportunities
- Captures their email for follow-up

This is the most important metric because the product’s purpose is not just traffic — it is generating high-intent leads for Credex.


---

## Input Metrics

| Metric | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Audit Completion Rate | Measures whether the landing page and form experience are effective |
| Email Capture Rate | Indicates whether users find enough value in the audit to save their report |
| Share Rate of Public Reports | Measures virality and organic distribution potential |

---

## What I’d Instrument First

- Landing page → audit start conversion
- Audit completion rate
- Time taken to complete an audit
- Email capture conversion
- Most selected AI tools/plans
- Average projected savings per audit
- Shareable report clicks

This would help identify where users drop off and which insights create the strongest engagement.

---

## Pivot Trigger

If the tool reaches:
- 1,000+ completed audits

but:
- Email capture stays below 10%
- Or consultation bookings stay below 3%

then the product likely provides curiosity value rather than strong purchase intent.

That would signal a need to:
- Improve the audit recommendations
- Increase perceived value of reports
- Reposition the product toward benchmarking or analytics instead of lead generation
