## FAQ

### 1. How does the audit work?
You enter the AI tools your team currently uses, your plans, and monthly spend. The tool analyzes your setup and recommends optimizations or cheaper alternatives.

### 2. Do I need to create an account?
No. The audit works instantly without signup. Email is only requested if you want to save or share your report.

### 3. Is the pricing data accurate?
Yes. All pricing data is sourced directly from official vendor pricing pages and regularly updated.

### 4. Does this only work for large companies?
No. The tool is designed for startups, indie hackers, agencies, and engineering teams of all sizes.

### 5. How does Credex help save money?
Credex provides discounted AI infrastructure credits and identifies opportunities to reduce unnecessary AI subscription spending.
