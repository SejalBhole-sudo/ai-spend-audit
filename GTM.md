# Go-To-Market Plan

## Exact Target User
Not "startups." The exact user is: **an engineering manager or CTO at a 5–30 person 
Series A or bootstrapped startup** who:
- Pays for 3+ AI tools simultaneously
- Has noticed their AI bill crossing $500/mo but hasn't audited it
- Reads Hacker News, follows AI tool launches on X
- Job title: "Head of Engineering", "CTO", "Founding Engineer", "VP Engineering"
- Company stage: post-PMF, pre-Series B (has money, but watching burn)

## What They Google Right Before Wanting This
- "cursor vs github copilot which is better"
- "is claude team worth it for small team"
- "AI tools for startups cost comparison 2025"
- "reduce SaaS spend startup"
- "chatgpt vs claude for coding team"

## Where They Hang Out Online
- **r/ExperiencedDevs**, **r/startups**, **r/SaaS** on Reddit
- Indie Hackers community (indiehackers.com/discuss)
- **Hacker News** — Show HN posts get massive traction for free tools
- **X/Twitter**: follow lists around @levelsio, @paulg, @swyx audiences
- Slack communities: LAUNCH (Jason Calacanis), MegaMaker, Ramen Club
- Discord: Buildspace, Developer DAO

## First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 Budget)
1. **Day 1-3: Show HN post** — "Show HN: I built a free tool that audits your AI tool spend" 
   — target 200+ upvotes, 50 users in 24 hours
2. **Day 4-7: Post in r/startups and r/ExperiencedDevs** — frame as "I found my team 
   was paying for 3 overlapping AI coding tools" personal story first
3. **Day 8-14: Cold DM 50 CTOs on X** — "Hey, built a free AI spend audit tool, 
   would love your feedback — takes 2 min" 
4. **Day 15-21: Indie Hackers post** — share the story of building it
5. **Day 22-30: Ask first 50 users to share** — the shareable URL IS the viral loop

## The Unfair Distribution Channel
Credex has existing relationships with AI tool buyers (that's the core business). 
Every enterprise sales conversation Credex has is with someone who is ALREADY 
overspending. The audit tool can be sent to every inbound Credex lead as a 
pre-qualification step — "run this first to see your savings" — before any sales call.

## Week-1 Traction (If It Works)
- 200+ audits completed within 72 hours of Show HN
- 40–60 email captures (20–30% capture rate)
- 5–10 high-savings leads (>$500/mo) flagged for Credex outreach
- 3+ organic shares on X from founders who found real savings
