# Why the Audit Tool Works

The AI Spend Audit is more than a lead magnet — it acts as a qualification engine for Credex.

Most developers and startups already spend heavily on AI tools but lack:

Visibility into total AI spend
Awareness of overlapping subscriptions
Clear ROI comparisons between tools

The audit identifies these inefficiencies while positioning Credex as the cheaper infrastructure layer for AI credits and subscriptions.

## This creates:

High-intent inbound leads
Lower acquisition costs
Better sales conversations than cold outreach

Credex does not create new AI spend — it redirects existing spend through a lower-cost procurement layer. This makes adoption easier because customers already budget for these tools.

## Core Business Thesis
CredexIQ succeeds if it becomes the default AI spend visibility layer for developers and startups.

As AI tooling adoption grows, teams increasingly struggle with:

Subscription overlap
Hidden AI costs
Poor spend visibility
Tool ROI comparison

If CredexIQ consistently helps users save money and optimize tool usage, the audit naturally becomes a scalable acquisition engine for Credex’s core business.
